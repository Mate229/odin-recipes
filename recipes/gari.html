Gari

Description

Gari here represents the soaked gari.

Gari soaking is a quick West African snack made by soaking gari 'cassava flakes' in cold water, then adding sugar, milk, and groundnuts. Refreshing and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of gari
  • 1 cup of cold water
  • 1 to 2 spoon of sugar
  • 2 to 3 spoons of milk
  • a handful of groundnuts
  • Optional:
    • coconut flakes
    • cubes

Steps

  1. Put gari in a bowl and add cold water to soak
  2. Let it absorb for 1 to 2 minutes
  3. Add sugar, milk, and groundnuts
  4. Stir well and enjoy immediately.

Bonus

You can enjoy your gari with a plate of Suya.

Bon appetit!
