Alloco

Description

In west Africa here, fried plaintains is called Alloco.

Ingredients

This is what you need to make alloco for 2:

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • Vegetable oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional:
    • Grillet fish
    • Meat
    • Spicy tomato sauce
    • omelette

Steps

This are the steps to make it:

  1. Slice ripe plantains into round pieces
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan
  3. Fry the skices until golden and crispy
  4. Drain, lightly salt, and serve hot with sauce, fish, or meat.

Bon appetit!
