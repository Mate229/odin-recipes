Suya

Description

Suya is a popular west african streed food made of thnly sliced, Spicy grilled meat, typically beef. Seasoned with a flavoured peanut based spice mix called yaji, it's grilled on skewers and served with onions, tomatoes, and something cabbage.

Ingredients

This is what it needs for 2 peoples:

  • 300g beef
  • 2 spoon of yaji
  • 10cl of vegetable oil
  • salt
  • Skewers
  • Sliced onions and tomatoes

Steps

This are the steps to make it:

  1. In a bowl, mix beef with oil, yaji, and a bit of salt
  2. Thred the beef onto skewers
  3. Grill or roast over hot charcoal for 15 to 20 mins, turning halfway
  4. Serve hot with sliced onions, tomatoes and extra yaji sprinkeld on top.

Bon appetit
